SMB tips — getting employees to embrace time tracking
Make it easy to use

Choose a time tracking system that is user-friendly and easy to navigate. Employees are more likely to adopt a system that's simple and straightforward.

Provide training & tutorials

Offer training sessions or tutorials to help employees understand how to use the system. This helps them feel more comfortable and confident from day one.

Set clear expectations

Communicate the importance of entering time accurately and on time. Let employees know how their time entries impact the company's overall performance.

Make it a habit

Encourage employees to make time tracking part of their daily routine. Set reminders or create a schedule to help them remember to log time regularly.
